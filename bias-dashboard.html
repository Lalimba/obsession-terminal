Daily Checklist

1. ETF flow positive or negative?
2. Coinbase premium rising or falling?
3. Funding neutral, overheated, or negative?
4. OI rising with price or falling with price?
5. Macro risk-on or risk-off?

Final bias rule example:
Bullish = ETF inflow + positive premium + neutral/negative funding + stable macro
Neutral = mixed flows + range price action
Defensive = ETF outflow + weak macro + long-crowded positioning
